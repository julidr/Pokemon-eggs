Stadistics

Whatch your hatching stadistics

This space allow you to literally whatch your hatching stadistics, like how many eggs of one Pokémon did you hatch or compare them with other Pokémon. You can also see a global result of all your hatched eggs and maybe other fancy stuff.

You have to load your Pokémon team info in My Eggs to see your stadistics.
